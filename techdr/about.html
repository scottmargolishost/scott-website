About Scott Margolis

Hi, I'm Scott. I have been helping the Chicagoland area for almost 20 years with their computer and home theater issues.

I am a local guy through and through. I grew up in Northbrook, where I still have family sprinkled throughout the North Shore. In 1995, I moved to Evanston and never looked back. Today, Skokie is my home base, but I travel all over the North Shore and Chicago to serve my clients.

I have a son named Charlie who was born in 2011, so I understand the importance of keeping a home network safe for family use!

My Philosophy

"No task is too small."

I know how frustrating technology can be. You just want it to work. My goal is to make your life easier and your time more efficient. Whether you need a full security audit of your home network, or you just want to know how to send a photo to your grandkids, I approach every job with the same level of patience and care.

I don't speak "geek." I speak plain English. My clients love that I explain why something happened and how to prevent it next time, rather than just fixing it silently and leaving.

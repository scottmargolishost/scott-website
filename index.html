The Backstory

Balancing the digital world
with the real one.

By trade, I'm the owner of Scott The Tech Dr., Inc., helping Chicago families and businesses navigate the complex digital world.

But this site isn't about work. Off the clock, I'm a dad to a high schooler and a man of many obsessions. I love exploring the Chicago food scene—whether it's trying new restaurants in the city and suburbs, or cooking at home with my girlfriend (and taking plenty of food pics along the way).

You'll often find me traveling, catching live music, watching my media on Plex, or riding my e-bike through the North Shore and Chicago. It's all about finding the perfect angle at a live show and the perfect bite in the neighborhood.
